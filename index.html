Innovation Project: Bubble Printer

Our innovation project introduces a captivating concept: the bubble printer. Through a fascinating blend of helium-infused soapy water and precision cut-outs, we've engineered a delightful spectable where bubbles take on diverse shapes and gracefully float away. Our aim is to captivate audiences and spark interest in 3D printing. Unlike traditional methods known for their expense and time consumption, our bubble printer is a whimiscal and accessible glimpse into the world of shape creation, allowing 3d printing to be more appriachable and engaging for all.
